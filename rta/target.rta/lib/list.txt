  :                            50 
  :                            51 .	this library is for a stream continuum
  :                            52 .	so nothing is set up to initialise
  :                            53 .	a field pointer of any kind
  :                            54 .	except it is a counted stream and must
  :                            55 .	do nothing if no bytes are available
  :                            56 .	and stop when it runs out of bytes
  :                            57 
  :                            58 .	this generation runs with the defaults
  :                            59 .	ASCII / UTF8, 8-bit byte
  :                            60 
  :                            61 .	the initialisation call READ_START
  :                            62 .	programs the staging registers
  :                            63 .	RDATAC+RDATA+Q to shift two bytes
  :                            64 .	per word from network buffers,
  :                            65 .	which store datalike this:
  :                            66 .	
  :                            67 .		_________________________________
  :                            68 .		| 0000 0000 XXXX xxxx XXXX xxxx |
  :                            69 .		|_______________________________|
  :                            70 
  :                            71 .	immediately on acquisition via register *q++
  :                            72 .	if bstream16r is on, RDATAC is decremented
  :                            73 .	from 24 to 16 and RDATA is shifted 8 bits
  :                            74 .	left:
  :                            75 
  :                            76 .		_________________________________
  :                            77 .		| XXXX xxxx XXXX xxxx 0000 0000 |
  :                            78 .		|_______________________________|
  :                            79 
  :                            80 
  :                            81 .	in contrast to 8 bit data in program data
  :                            82 .	which uses all 24 bits in the data word:
  :                            83 
  :                            84 .		_________________________________
  :                            85 .		| XXXX xxxx XXXX xxxx XXXX xxxx |
  :                            86 .		|_______________________________|
  :                            87 
  :                            88 .	the staging registers deliver or output
  :                            89 .	16 bits per word if PSR flags
  :                            90 
  :                            91 .		b$stream16r
  :                            92 .		b$stream16w
  :                            93 
  :                            94 .	are on, otherwise 24 bits per word
