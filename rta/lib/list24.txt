  :                            53 		. indicates the end of a token
  :                            54 
  :                            55 		. LAST_BYTE_READ is pushed back into RDATA
  :                            56 		. so that the next scan primitive routine
  :                            57 		. does not have to look for it
  :                            58 
  :                            59 		. and a saved stream complex of registers
  :                            60 		. RDATAC+RDATA+Q does not need another
  :                            61 		. variable in addition
  :                            62 
  :                            63 		. in a counted stream it does no harm
  :                            64 		. to increase BYTES from 0 to 1 after the
  :                            65 		. last real symbol has been consumed
  :                            66 
  :                            67 		. the next read will produce the dummy
  :                            68 		. value of zero again, the next scan will
  :                            69 		. terminate because of that, and BYTES
  :                            70 		. will go back to zero
  :                            71 
  :                            72 		. COUNTED_STREAM is the text scan inside
  :                            73 		. a field which has a known size instead
  :                            74 		. of an expected null terminator
  :                            75

  :                            77 		. or any fixed field conversion from text
