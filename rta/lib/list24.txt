  :                            61 		. variable in addition

  :                            64 		. to increase BYTES from 0 to 1 after the
  :                            65 		. last real symbol has been consumed

  :                            68 		. value of zero again, the next scan will
  :                            69 		. terminate because of that, and BYTES
  :                            70 		. will go back to zero

  :                            73 		. a field which has a known size instead
  :                            74 		. of an expected null terminator
